Privacy Policy

In my opinion, every person deserves the right to privacy. To reflect that, this website does not track, gather information about, or profile its users in any way. Feel free to browse this website without limits; your activity will be completely unmonitored. However, this website contains links to external websites and streaming services with their own privacy policies. I cannot accept responsibility for the privacy policies of external websites, so be advised to click links to third-party websites at your own discretion.

Below is a list of invasive technologies you will not find on this website.

  • IP logging
  • Analytics
  • Third-party advertising
